Privacy Policy

Voice 2 TXT Memo • Last updated: June 19, 2026

TL;DR: Voice 2 TXT Memo processes everything on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

None. Voice 2 TXT Memo does not collect any personal information. All voice transcription is performed using Apple's on-device speech recognition engine. Your voice recordings and transcribed memos never leave your device.

2. Data Storage

All memos you create are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Microphone & Speech Recognition

The app requires microphone access and speech recognition permission solely to convert your speech to text in real time. Audio is processed entirely on-device by Apple's Speech framework. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio.

4. Third-Party Services

Voice 2 TXT Memo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

Voice 2 TXT Memo does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no account system, no tracking, and no data collection of any kind.

7. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete any or all memos directly within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all associated data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
