Voice 2 TXT Memo • Help Center

Getting Started

1. Open Voice 2 TXT Memo and tap the microphone button at the bottom of the screen.

2. Grant microphone and speech recognition permissions when prompted.

3. Start speaking — your words will appear in real time as text.

4. Tap the stop button to save your memo. It will appear in your memo list with a timestamp.

Multi-Language Support

Tap the flag icon in the top-left corner to select from 30+ languages. Your language preference is saved automatically.

Managing Memos

  • Share a memo by tapping the share icon on any memo card
  • Delete a memo by tapping the trash icon (with confirmation)
  • Export all memos as a text file using the export button in the top-right corner

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app need an internet connection?

A: No. All speech recognition is performed on-device. An internet connection is not required.

Q: Is my voice data stored or sent anywhere?

A: No. All audio processing happens locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

Q: How do I recover deleted memos?

A: Deleted memos cannot be recovered. A confirmation dialog is shown before deletion to prevent accidents.

Q: Speech recognition isn't working. What should I do?

A: Go to Settings → Voice 2 TXT Memo and ensure both Microphone and Speech Recognition permissions are enabled. Restart the app after granting permissions.
